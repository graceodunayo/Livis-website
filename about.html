About Us

LIVIS MAISON is a luxury fashion brand that offers high-quality clothing and accessories.

Our mission is to provide our customers with stylish and sophisticated designs that make them feel confident and elegant.

We believe in the importance of fashion and its ability to bring people together.

Our team of designers and stylists work tirelessly to create unique and timeless pieces that are both beautiful and functional.

Livis Maison Enterprise is a leading clothing brand providing high-quality and stylish apparel for men. Our mission is to bring fashion to every man at affordable prices.

Our Design Inspiration

At LIVIS MAISON, our designs are inspired by a blend of African heritage and contemporary global trends. We take pride in our Senator Style—a modern interpretation of traditional menswear, known for its clean lines, rich fabrics, and elegant embroidery. Each piece is thoughtfully crafted to celebrate individuality and cultural pride.

Our creative process starts with listening to our clients. We believe that every outfit should tell a story, whether it's a bespoke suit for a special occasion or a casual ensemble for everyday confidence. Our designers work closely with skilled tailors to ensure every garment is a perfect fit, both in style and comfort.

Quality is at the heart of everything we do. From fabric selection to finishing touches, we are committed to delivering clothing that stands the test of time. Our collections feature bold colors, innovative cuts, and unique details that set LIVIS MAISON apart in the world of fashion.

Discover the difference of LIVIS MAISON—where tradition meets innovation, and every design is made just for you.

Our Journey

  • 2015: LIVIS MAISON is founded with a vision to redefine men's fashion in Africa.
  • 2017: Launch of our first Senator collection, blending tradition and modernity.
  • 2019: Expansion into custom tailoring and personal shopping services.
  • 2022: Recognized as a leading brand for quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.
  • 2025: Continues to set trends and inspire confidence in men across the globe.
